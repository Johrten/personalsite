About Me

Hey there, welcome to my website! My name is Johrten, and I'm a senior technical product manager with a passion for creating awesome products that users love. Before I got into product management, I spent a bunch of years as a developer, working with JavaScript and AWS to build apps that made a ton of money. But, I realized that what I really wanted to do was work closely with others to make amazing products. So, I made the switch.

Now, as a product manager, I use data and design to roadmap products that are not only functional but also user-friendly. I take pride in my ability to communicate effectively with cross-functional teams, and I'm always looking for new ways to make sure that the end-users are at the forefront of every decision we make.

Lately, I've been fascinated by AI tools like ChatGPT and Github CoPilot, and I've been experimenting with integrating AI into my own workflow. I'm excited about the potential of these technologies to revolutionize how we approach product development and problem-solving in creative ways.

So, whether you're here to learn more about me, my work, or just to see what kind of drink I prefer (spoiler alert: it's a spicy margarita), you're in the right place. If you're ever in Los Angeles and want to grab one, let me know!
