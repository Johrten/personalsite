About
Me

I recently graduated from the Flatiron School's 15-week full-stack development course. While attending the program, I used Ruby (on Rails), pure Javascript, and React.js to build a variety of programs that took advantage of RESTful APIs. Additionally, I taught myself how to use WebSockets and learned recently released tools such as React Context API.

Prior to my time at Flatiron, I was the Spa Director at Gentle Healing Wellness Spa. I was responsible for all day-to-day operations of the business, including staff management, marketing initiatives, and print/online design.

In Spring of 2016, I graduated from New York University's Gallatin School in three years with a Bachelor of Arts in Content Creation and Marketing. Additionally, I received a minor in the business of entertainment, media and technology. By taking classes over summer and winter breaks, I received my diploma a full year ahead of schedule. Some of the classes I took were "Production and Publication," “Media Ethics & First Amendment Law” and "Managing Creative Content Development."
